Description

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese.

Shred and grate the cheese from a block, avoid using packaged shredded/grated cheese for this recipe. Low moisture, whole milk mozzarella cheese melts the best.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. ¾ cup Parmesan cheese
  2. 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  3. ½ teaspoon salt
